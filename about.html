About myself

Welcome to my portfolio! Growing up I always had a creative passion and I love building things working with my hands also learning new things. I love the outdoors and the indoors when the outdoors isn’t looking so pretty. Born October 23rd, 1997, in Peterborough, Ontario with a perspective of a Scorpio I had some trouble learning the hard way, but I find learning the hard way is the best way because that gives you the truth. I see truth brings hard work. I always had a vision for more or big but to this day I still struggle with figuring that out exactly. For the last 5 months, I've built myself an online reputation on mostly TikTok of my hometown (Peterborough).

It started off with just boredom and it's funny to think about how many cool interests and facts my hometown had and seemed to me no one else knew about. I've been posting 2 to 3 times weekly since and I'm on the track to getting 8,000 followers! It’s been a journey so far meeting people here in Ottawa who've recognized me. People tell me all the time they love my videos. It’s a great conversation starter, I think. But it also gives me content creator experience which I can’t complain about.

I work security and if you have worked security, you know how much free time you have when you work these types of shifts. I also have gotten into reading and listening to podcasts because I find it more entertaining than watching pointless shows all the time and not learning anything valuable. We as humans waste so much time choosing pleasure over the practice. My goal for myself is to one day bring an impact to the world, I don’t know what it is yet or how I will do it, but I can feel in myself that it will happen. I believe in kindness and empathy, loyalty, respect, protection, and providing.
