About Myself

Hey there, I'm Thom and this is my portfolio! Since I was a kid, I've been all about creativity and building stuff with my hands, since Algonquin College I've discovered this was the career I want. Always hungry for new knowledge, too. I'm a fan of both the outdoors and cozy indoor, rain or shine.

My willing to learn and grow perspective led me to some challenging lessons, yet I've come to appreciate that the hard-earned lessons often reveal the truth, paving the way for diligent work. From an early age, I harbored grand visions, although the precise realization of these aspirations remains an ongoing challenge. Over the past year, I've meticulously curated an online presence, predominantly on TikTok and YouTube, showcasing beautiful Ontario and Mordern Dating Life Adivce. What began as a product of boredom metamorphosed into a repository of fascinating local interests and facts that seemed to elude others. Posting weekly, I've steadily amassed a following, approaching the 20,000 mark.

Which has lead me to my new journey on YouTube, to mordern dating life coach. I'm very passioante when it comes to mordern dating and how it moves now. Occupationally, I am engaged in the realm of security, a profession notorious for its surplus of free time during unconventional shifts. Capitalizing on these moments, I've delved into reading and podcast listening, reading books, finding greater entertainment value and knowledge. Recognizing the prevalent human tendency to prioritize pleasure over meaningful pursuits, I strive to optimize my time.

In the future, I aim to leave a positive mark on the world. Although I'm not yet certain about the exact way or form this will take, I have a strong inner belief that it will happen. My values revolve around kindness, empathy, loyalty, respect, protection, and making a meaningful contribution.
